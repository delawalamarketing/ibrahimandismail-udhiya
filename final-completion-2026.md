### FAQ answer — final copy (use verbatim)

**Q: Is this qurbaani valid for all four madhahib (Hanafi, Shafi'i, Maliki, Hanbali)?**

A: Yes. Our process meets the requirements of all four madhahib:
- Performed by a practising Muslim slaughterman
- "Bismillah, Allahu Akbar" recited at the moment of slaughter, with your name and intention noted
- Single swipe of a sharp, non-serrated blade severing jugular veins, carotid arteries, windpipe, and esophagus
- **No stunning of any kind** — animals are fully conscious at the moment of sacrifice
- Carried out at an HMA-certified, OMAFRA-licensed Ontario facility under provincial food-safety inspection

**Q: Where does the slaughter happen?**

A: At our Ontario farm partner — an HMA-certified, OMAFRA-licensed facility located approximately one hour from the GTA. Provincial food-safety inspectors are on site during every sacrifice.
